About

Hi, I'm Tommy! I am a PhD Candidate in Cognitive Neuroscience at Dartmouth College (Department of Psychological and Brain Sciences), primarily working with Emily Finn.

My research aims to understand how humans process and make predictions about language. I am particularly interested in how and when language representation diverges both between individuals and between humans and artificial neural networks (e.g., large language models). To tackle these questions, I use a combination of human behavior, functional neuroimaging (fMRI), and machine learning.

Before starting my PhD, I completed a B.S. in Psychobiology at UCLA and worked as a lab manager for Caroline Robertson at Dartmouth College.
